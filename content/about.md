## Profile

### Overview

Product and technology leader with 10+ years of building secure, privacy preserving products in regulated industries. Deeply embedded in the digital identity landscape, building infrastructure and platform for Verifiable Digital Credentials (VDC). Currently Chief Product Officer at Meeco, where I manage a distributed team of 5-15 engineers, product managers and test engineers across multiple time zones (EU and Australia). In this role I drive product strategy, platform architecture, and international projects.

Deep expertise in standards-based systems (FIDO, IETF, ISO, OIDF, W3C), regulatory compliance (eIDAS, GDPR, ISO 27001) and building systems at global scale (Australia, EU, Japan). Active contributor to OpenID Foundation DCP working group and speaker at the European Identity Conference (EIC).

Thrives in dynamic, startup-like environments where there is freedom to innovate. My first love is software where I can bring deep technical expertise, but my expertise extends to strategy, business, and management.

I'm used to working in an international environment, with people from different cultures and backgrounds from all over the world. I don't mind speaking engagements and enjoy sharing my thoughts in written form on this website and LinkedIn.

## Professional Experience

### Chief Product Officer @ Meeco (2019 - now)

**Strategic Leadership**

- Conceptualised and delivered [SVX platform](https://www.meeco.me/platform) (2022-present): verifiable digital credentials platform enabling issuance, presentation, verification, and operation of credential schemes. Deployed and operational across EU and Japan.
- Architected out-of-the-box identity verification solution using verifiable credentials, currently [being trialed in Japan](https://www.dnp.co.jp/news/detail/20177806_1587.html) (Tokyo, Niseko) and [South Korea](https://youtu.be/XVw-9osdVpE) for cross-border identity verification use cases.
- Drive 1-5 year strategic planning, aligning product vision with global digital identity regulatory landscapes (e.g. eIDAS 2.0, TDIF).

**Team Leadership and Development**

- Manage a team of 5-15 members across multiple time zones, fostering a culture of collaboration.
- Develop a repeatable process for designing and delivering high-quality products. The process uses design and planning documents, reviews, and feedback sessions that work in a remote-first, asynchronous environment.
- Use a combination of traditional and agile methodologies to ensure efficient and effective project delivery.
- Mentored and coached team members to enhance their technical and leadership skills.

**Technical and Regulatory Expertise**

- Apply standards from FIDO Alliance, IETF, ISO, OpenID Foundation, and W3C to ensure interoperability and compliance. Successfully [participated](https://openid.net/haip-1-0-openid4vp-1-0-achieve-98-in-oidf-interop-testing/) in interoperability testing and certification programs.
- Lead research and proof-of-concept initiatives exploring agentic commerce (using [AP2](https://ap2-protocol.org/)), IC card integration with VDCs (NFC stack), bridging traditional account-based systems (e.g. OIDC) and VDCs, and integrating payments with verifiable credentials.
- Validate implementations against regulatory frameworks and standards such as [eIDAS 2.0 implementation acts](https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/spaces/EUDIGITALIDENTITYWALLET/pages/915931811/The+European+Digital+Identity+Regulation?filters=adopted&all=1#sec-6-regulations), GDPR compliance, [AGDIS](https://www.digitalidsystem.gov.au/), and other relevant regulations in environments where Meeco and its partners are operating.
